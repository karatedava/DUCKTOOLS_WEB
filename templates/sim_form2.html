Optimize your long-term duckweed cultivation (30+ days) with precise setup details. This simulator computes the optimal harvesting strategy and estimated cultivation time tailored to your desired yield.

For short-term cultivation, try the General Simulator to find optimal parameters.

How It Works

  • YieldPredictor generates an initial prediction of the optimal cultivation strategy.
  • Cultivation time is estimated based on your inputs.
  • GrowthModeler refines the strategy for the estimated cultivation time.
  • The required cultivation time to achieve your desired yield is calculated.
